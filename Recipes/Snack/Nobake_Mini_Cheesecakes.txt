No-Bake Mini Cheesecakes
# 
6 Mini Cheesecakes
# 
265 minutes
#
No-bake and cheesecake are two of our favorite words. Put them together and you have this tasty treat that is delicious on its own as well as the perfect companion to your favorite toppings.
#
Cream_Cheese_Softened 4.0 Ounces
Whipped_Topping 4.0 Ounces
Sour_Cream_Room_Temperature  0.5 Cup
Granulated_Sugar  0.75 Cup
Vanilla_Extract 1.0 Teaspoon
Vanilla_Wafers 6.0
Strawberries 6.0
#
1. In a medium bowl, mix cream cheese, whipped topping, sour cream, sugar, and vanilla until well combined

2. Place 6 foil cupcake liners in a cupcake pan. Put a cookie in the bottom of each, cutting them to fit if necessary.

3. Divide the batter evenly among cupcake liners. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours.

4. Top each cheesecake with a strawberry.
