Chicken Salad Sandwich 
#
1 Sandwich
#
7 minutes
#
This is a great use for leftover cooked chicken breast.
#
Chicken_Canned 1.0
Celery_Minced 120.0 g
Salt
Paprika
Mayonnaise 14.4 g
Raisin_Bread 2.0 Slice
Provolone_Cheese 2.0 Slice
Apple 0.25
#
1. Place chicken in a small bowl. Add celery, salt, pa-prika, and mayonnaise, Mix until combined.

2. On a slice of bread, layer a slice of cheese, half of the apple slices, chicken mixture, the remaining apple slices, and the other slice of cheese. Close the sandwich and wrap in plastic wrap until lunch.
