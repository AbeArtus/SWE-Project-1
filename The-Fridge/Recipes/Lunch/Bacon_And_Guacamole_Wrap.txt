Bacon and Guacamole Wrap
# 
1 Wrap
#
7 minutes
#
Avocado: incredibly healthy. Bacon: not so healthy. Avocado and bacon together: incredibly delicious.
#
Guacamole 50.0 g
Tortilla 1.0
Pepper_Jack_Cheese 3.0 Slice
Bacon_Cooked 3.0 Slice
#
1. Spread guacamole on tortilla. Layer cheese on top of guacamole.

2. Place bacon on top of cheese. Fold the top and bottom of the tortilla toward the middle. Then roll the tortilla from left to right to close. Wrap in plastic wrap until ready to eat.
